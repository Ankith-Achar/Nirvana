Bipolar Disorder Assessment

This questionnaire is designed to help assess symptoms commonly associated with Bipolar Disorder. Please answer each question honestly, considering if you have ever experienced these symptoms during a distinct period when you were not your usual self.

For questions 2-12, rate each item on a scale from 0 (Not at all) to 5 (Extremely/Almost always).

Important: This is not a diagnostic tool. If you're experiencing mood-related symptoms, please consult a mental health professional.

1. Has there ever been a period of time when you were not your usual self and felt so good or hyper that other people thought you were not your normal self or were so hyper that you got into trouble?

2. During such periods, how often were you so irritable that you shouted at people or started fights or arguments?

3. During such periods, how often did you feel much more self-confident than usual?

4. During such periods, how often did you get much less sleep than usual and found you didn't really miss it?

5. During such periods, how often were you much more talkative or spoke much faster than usual?

6. During such periods, how often did thoughts race through your head or you couldn't slow your mind down?

7. During such periods, how often were you so easily distracted by things around you that you had trouble concentrating or staying on track?

8. During such periods, how often did you have much more energy than usual?

9. During such periods, how often were you much more social or outgoing than usual, for example, you telephoned friends in the middle of the night?

10. During such periods, how often were you much more interested in sex than usual?

11. During such periods, how often did you do things that were unusual for you or that other people might have thought were excessive, foolish, or risky?

12. During such periods, how often did spending money get you or your family into trouble?
