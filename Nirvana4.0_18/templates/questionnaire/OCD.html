OCD Assessment

This questionnaire is designed to help assess symptoms commonly associated with Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD). Please answer each question honestly, considering how you've felt over the past two weeks.

Rate each item on a scale from 0 (Not at all) to 5 (Extremely/Almost always).

Important: This is not a diagnostic tool. If you're experiencing distress, please consult a mental health professional.

1. How often have you been bothered by unwanted and intrusive thoughts that cause anxiety?

2. How difficult has it been to stop or control these unwanted thoughts?

3. How often have you felt compelled to perform certain behaviors or mental acts repeatedly?

4. How much distress do you experience when unable to perform your rituals or compulsions?

5. How much time do you spend on obsessive thoughts or compulsive behaviors each day?

6. How concerned are you about cleanliness, contamination, or germs?

7. How often do you need to check things repeatedly (e.g., locks, switches, appliances)?

8. How strong is your need for symmetry, order, or exactness?

9. How often are you troubled by unwanted thoughts about harm coming to yourself or others?

10. How often do you collect or have difficulty discarding items that most people would consider useless?

11. How often do you feel the need to count, tap, or repeat certain words/phrases?

12. How strong is your need for reassurance from others?

13. How concerned are you with doing things "just right" or perfectly?

14. How difficult is it for you to make everyday decisions?

15. How much have these symptoms interfered with your daily life, work, or relationships?
