Depression Assessment (PHQ-9)

This questionnaire (PHQ-9) is designed to help assess symptoms commonly associated with depression. Please answer each question honestly, considering how you've felt over the past two weeks.

Important: This is not a diagnostic tool. If you're experiencing distress, please consult a mental health professional.

1. Little interest or pleasure in doing things

2. Feeling down, depressed, or hopeless

3. Trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much

4. Feeling tired or having little energy

5. Poor appetite or overeating

6. Feeling bad about yourself - or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down

7. Trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television

8. Moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed
Or the opposite - being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual

9. Thoughts that you would be better off dead, or of hurting yourself

10. If you checked off any problems, how difficult have these problems made it for you at work, home, or with other people?
